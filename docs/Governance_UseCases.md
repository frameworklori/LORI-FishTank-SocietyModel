# Governance Use Cases

## English

The framework can support non-commercial education, policy discussion, classroom simulation, and systems-thinking exercises.

Possible use cases:

- Explaining liquidity stress through oxygen shortage
- Explaining inflation pressure through temperature change
- Explaining trust collapse through water quality degradation
- Comparing overfeeding and underfeeding policy failures
- Teaching why crisis prevention requires multiple indicators
- Mapping how resource concentration harms system resilience

The model should not be used as an official policy engine or financial forecasting tool.

## 繁體中文

此框架可用於非商業教育、政策討論、課堂模擬與系統思維練習。

可能用途：

- 以氧氣不足解釋流動性壓力
- 以水溫變化解釋通膨壓力
- 以水質惡化解釋信任崩潰
- 比較過度餵食與餵食不足的政策失敗
- 教學說明危機預防為何需要多重指標
- 對照資源集中如何傷害系統韌性

此模型不應被用作官方政策引擎或金融預測工具。
