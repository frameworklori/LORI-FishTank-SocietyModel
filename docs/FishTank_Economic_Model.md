# FishTank Economic Model

## English

The FishTank Global Economic Simulation System uses a fish tank as a symbolic model of global economic dynamics.

The tank is the economy. The fish are economic actors. Food is monetary and resource input. Water quality is trust. Oxygen is liquidity. Temperature is inflation, interest rate pressure, and geopolitical stress.

The core idea is that economic crises rarely emerge from one variable alone. They often form when liquidity, trust, resource distribution, behavior, and external shocks interact inside a bounded system.

This model is not designed to forecast exact market prices. It is designed to identify imbalance patterns, systemic fragility, and early crisis signals.

## 繁體中文

FishTank 全球經濟模擬系統以魚缸作為全球經濟動態的象徵模型。

魚缸代表經濟體。魚代表經濟參與者。飼料代表貨幣與資源投放。水質代表信任。氧氣代表流動性。水溫代表通膨、利率壓力與地緣政治壓力。

核心概念是：經濟危機很少只由單一變數形成。危機通常是在流動性、信任、資源分配、行為反應與外部衝擊於封閉系統中互相作用時逐漸形成。

此模型不是用於預測精準市場價格，而是用於識別失衡模式、系統脆弱性與早期危機訊號。
