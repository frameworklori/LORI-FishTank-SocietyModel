# Risk Assessment

## English

The FishTank Global Economic Simulation System uses ecological variables to evaluate whether an economic system is approaching instability. The purpose is not to predict exact market prices, but to identify imbalance patterns, systemic fragility, and early crisis signals.

## 繁體中文

FishTank 全球經濟模擬系統透過魚缸生態變數，評估一個經濟系統是否正在接近失衡。此系統並非用於精準預測市場價格，而是用於識別失衡模式、系統脆弱性與早期危機訊號。

## Risk Categories

### 1. Liquidity Risk

English: Too little oxygen in the tank. Represents liquidity shortage, credit freeze, and banking stress.

繁體中文：魚缸中的氧氣過少。代表流動性短缺、信用凍結與銀行壓力。

### 2. Overfeeding Risk

English: Too much fish food. Represents excessive money supply, QE, stimulus abuse, asset bubbles, and moral hazard.

繁體中文：魚飼料過多。代表貨幣供給過度、量化寬鬆、刺激政策濫用、資產泡沫與道德風險。

### 3. Water Quality Risk

English: Dirty or unstable water. Represents loss of trust, institutional opacity, corruption, misinformation, and broken transparency.

繁體中文：水質骯髒或不穩定。代表信任流失、制度不透明、腐敗、錯誤資訊與透明度破裂。

### 4. Temperature Shock Risk

English: Sudden change in water temperature. Represents inflation spike, interest rate shock, energy shock, and geopolitical shock.

繁體中文：水溫突然變化。代表通膨飆升、利率衝擊、能源衝擊與地緣政治衝擊。

### 5. Behavioral Panic Risk

English: Fish panic or crowding. Represents bank runs, market panic, herd behavior, and social instability.

繁體中文：魚群恐慌或群聚。代表銀行擠兌、市場恐慌、羊群行為與社會不穩。

### 6. Resource Misallocation Risk

English: Uneven feeding distribution. Represents unequal subsidies, wealth concentration, zombie companies, and unfair capital access.

繁體中文：餵食分配不均。代表補貼不均、財富集中、殭屍企業與資本取得不公平。

### 7. Systemic Collapse Risk

English: Multiple tank variables fail together. Represents financial crisis, recession spiral, sovereign debt crisis, and institutional breakdown.

繁體中文：多個魚缸變數同時失效。代表金融危機、衰退螺旋、主權債務危機與制度崩解。

## Risk Scoring Model

Risk Score = Probability x Impact x Spread Factor x Recovery Difficulty

## 風險評分模型

風險分數 = 發生機率 x 影響程度 x 擴散因子 x 復原困難度

## Example Interpretation

English: A tank with low oxygen, dirty water, rising temperature, and panic behavior may produce a high or severe risk score even if food supply still appears sufficient.

繁體中文：即使飼料供給看似充足，若魚缸同時出現氧氣不足、水質骯髒、水溫上升與魚群恐慌，也可能形成高風險或嚴重風險分數。
