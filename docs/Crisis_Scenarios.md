# Crisis Scenarios

## English

### Overfeeding Crisis

Too much food enters the tank. Fish initially appear active and healthy, but excess food decays, water quality declines, and competition becomes distorted.

Economic meaning: excessive credit, stimulus abuse, leverage, asset bubbles, speculation, moral hazard.

### Underfeeding Recession

Too little food enters the tank. Fish reduce movement, competition intensifies, weaker fish fail first, and the tank becomes socially unstable.

Economic meaning: unemployment, recession, demand collapse, credit contraction, social unrest.

### Water Quality Trust Collapse

The tank appears physically intact, but the water becomes dirty and unreadable. Fish lose orientation and begin panic behavior.

Economic meaning: corruption, opacity, misinformation, institutional distrust, bank runs, market panic.

### Temperature Shock

Water temperature changes too quickly. Even if food and oxygen exist, fish become stressed because the environment shifts faster than adaptation capacity.

Economic meaning: inflation spike, interest rate shock, energy shock, geopolitical disruption.

## 繁體中文

### 過度餵食危機

過多飼料進入魚缸。魚群一開始看似活躍健康，但多餘飼料腐敗，水質下降，競爭關係開始扭曲。

經濟含義：信用過度、刺激政策濫用、槓桿、資產泡沫、投機、道德風險。

### 餵食不足衰退

過少飼料進入魚缸。魚群活動減少，競爭加劇，弱勢魚群最先失敗，魚缸變得不穩定。

經濟含義：失業、衰退、需求崩塌、信用收縮、社會不安。

### 水質信任崩潰

魚缸表面上仍完整，但水變得骯髒且難以辨識。魚群失去方向感並開始恐慌。

經濟含義：腐敗、不透明、錯誤資訊、制度不信任、銀行擠兌、市場恐慌。

### 水溫衝擊

水溫變化過快。即使仍有飼料與氧氣，魚群也會因環境變化超過適應能力而承受壓力。

經濟含義：通膨飆升、利率衝擊、能源衝擊、地緣政治擾動。
