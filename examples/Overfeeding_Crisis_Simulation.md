# Overfeeding Crisis Simulation

## English

Scenario: Too much food enters the tank too quickly.

Economic meaning: Excessive money supply, QE abuse, stimulus dependency, debt expansion, asset bubbles, leverage, greed, and moral hazard.

Simulation pattern:

1. Food input rises above sustainable absorption.
2. Fish behavior becomes aggressive and speculative.
3. Uneaten food decays and damages water quality.
4. Trust declines as visible prosperity becomes unstable.
5. Panic emerges when oxygen and water quality fall together.

Boundary: This scenario is symbolic and educational. It is not a market forecast.

## 繁體中文

情境：過多飼料太快進入魚缸。

經濟含義：貨幣供給過度、量化寬鬆濫用、刺激政策依賴、債務擴張、資產泡沫、槓桿、貪婪與道德風險。

模擬模式：

1. 飼料投入超過可持續吸收能力。
2. 魚群行為變得激烈且投機。
3. 未被消耗的飼料腐敗並破壞水質。
4. 當表面繁榮變得不穩定時，信任下降。
5. 當氧氣與水質同時下降時，恐慌出現。

邊界：此情境屬象徵性與教育性內容，不是市場預測。
