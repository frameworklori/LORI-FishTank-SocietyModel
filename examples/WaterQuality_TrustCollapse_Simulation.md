# Water Quality Trust Collapse Simulation

## English

Scenario: The tank still contains food and fish, but the water becomes dirty, opaque, and unstable.

Economic meaning: Corruption, opacity, misinformation, institutional distrust, broken transparency, bank runs, and market panic.

Simulation pattern:

1. Water quality declines gradually.
2. Fish lose orientation even when resources are still present.
3. Panic behavior increases because the environment becomes unreadable.
4. Trust-based coordination fails.
5. Crisis accelerates when liquidity and confidence fall together.

Boundary: This scenario models trust collapse symbolically. It is not an official economic forecast.

## 繁體中文

情境：魚缸中仍有飼料與魚，但水變得骯髒、不透明且不穩定。

經濟含義：腐敗、不透明、錯誤資訊、制度不信任、透明度破裂、銀行擠兌與市場恐慌。

模擬模式：

1. 水質逐步下降。
2. 即使資源仍存在，魚群也失去方向感。
3. 因環境變得難以辨識，恐慌行為增加。
4. 以信任為基礎的協調失效。
5. 當流動性與信心同時下降時，危機加速。

邊界：此情境以象徵方式模擬信任崩潰，不是官方經濟預測。
