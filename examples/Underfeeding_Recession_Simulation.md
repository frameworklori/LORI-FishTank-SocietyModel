# Underfeeding Recession Simulation

## English

Scenario: Too little food enters the tank for too long.

Economic meaning: Unemployment, recession, demand collapse, credit contraction, shrinking investment, and social unrest.

Simulation pattern:

1. Food input falls below survival needs.
2. Fish activity decreases.
3. Competition for limited food intensifies.
4. Weaker fish fail first.
5. Trust and social stability decline as scarcity spreads.

Boundary: This scenario illustrates recession dynamics conceptually. It is not financial advice.

## 繁體中文

情境：太少飼料長時間進入魚缸。

經濟含義：失業、衰退、需求崩塌、信用收縮、投資縮減與社會不安。

模擬模式：

1. 飼料投入低於生存需求。
2. 魚群活動下降。
3. 對有限飼料的競爭加劇。
4. 弱勢魚群最先失敗。
5. 當稀缺擴散時，信任與社會穩定下降。

邊界：此情境以概念方式說明衰退動態，不是財務建議。
